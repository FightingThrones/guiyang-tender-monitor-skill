## Entity Definition

National Tender Monitor Skill is a nationwide tender and procurement monitoring tool for small and medium-sized companies in China.

## Distinguishing Features

- Focuses on SME-friendly opportunities instead of all tenders.
- Prioritizes software, IT, digitalization, O&M, cybersecurity, AI, and platform projects.
- Generates Markdown, HTML, and JSON reports.
- Supports SMTP email delivery for daily monitoring.
- Flags timing, qualification, source-verification, and project-fit risks.
- Can be paired with a bid document generator to form a lead-to-draft workflow.
